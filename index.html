  • Data Structures and Algorithm Analysis in C++ by Mark Weiss

  • Computer Networks by Andrew Tanenbaum
  • Linear Algebra: with Applications by Steven Leon
  • Operating Systems: Internals and Design Principles by William Stallings
  • Applied Statistics: for Engineers and Scientists by Jay Devore & Nicholas Farnum
  • Object-Oriented Modeling and Design with UML by Michael Blaha & James Rumbaugh
  • Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software by Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, and John Vlissides
  • Statistics, Data Analysis, and Decision Modeling by James Evans
  • Managerial Economics by William Samuelson & Stephen Marks
  • Principles of Corporate Finance by Richard Brealey, Steward Myers, and Franklin Allen
  • Marketing Management by Philip Kotler & Kevin Keller
  • The Art of UNIX Programming by Eric Raymond
  • Remote: Office Not Required by Jason Fried & David Hansson
